## Ongoing projects

### Moonfern

MoonFern is a 2D narrative adventure game made in Godot, centered around a healer witch with a deep affinity for herbology, memory, and the living forest. The player follows a young woman from a forest-dwelling people whose survival depends on staying hidden within the woods. When a bright, mechanical race of bladed-arm mannequins begins hunting her community, she must lead the attackers away from her encampment while protecting and healing the forest by brewing potions and taking them to the trees to heal them.
